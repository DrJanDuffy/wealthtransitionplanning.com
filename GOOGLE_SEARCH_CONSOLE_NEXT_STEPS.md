## Troubleshooting

### If Pages Aren't Indexing:
1. Check **Coverage** report for errors
2. Verify pages are accessible (no 404 errors)
3. Check robots.txt isn't blocking pages
4. Ensure pages have quality content

### If Sitemap Shows Errors:
1. Verify sitemap is accessible at `/sitemap.xml`
2. Check that all URLs in sitemap are valid
3. Ensure `NEXT_PUBLIC_BASE_URL` is set correctly
