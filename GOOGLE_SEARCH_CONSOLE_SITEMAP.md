Add this sitemap URL to Google Search Console:


---

## How to Add Sitemap to Google Search Console

### Step 1: Access Google Search Console
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console)
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Select your property: `wealthtransitionplanning.com` or `www.wealthtransitionplanning.com`

### Step 2: Submit Sitemap
1. In the left sidebar, click **"Sitemaps"**
2. In the "Add a new sitemap" field, enter:
   ```
   sitemap.xml
   ```
   (Google will automatically prepend your domain)
3. Click **"Submit"**

### Step 3: Verify
- Google will process your sitemap
- Status should show "Success" within a few minutes
- You'll see the number of URLs discovered

## Troubleshooting

### If Sitemap Shows Errors:
1. **404 Error**: Make sure the site is deployed and accessible
2. **Empty Sitemap**: Check that pages are being generated correctly
3. **Wrong Domain**: Verify `NEXT_PUBLIC_BASE_URL` is set correctly in Vercel
